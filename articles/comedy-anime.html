15 Must-Watch Comedy Anime Series

Published: October 15, 2023

Introduction

Looking for a good laugh? These comedy anime series offer the perfect blend of humor, entertainment, and memorable characters that will keep you laughing throughout each episode.

15. Haven't You Heard? I'm Sakamoto

Follow the impossibly perfect Sakamoto as he handles every situation with unbelievable style, creating absurd and hilarious scenarios that will leave you in stitches.

14. Mob Psycho 100

A powerful psychic boy tries to live a normal life while working for a fraudulent psychic, leading to both amazing action and great comedy.

13. Devil is a Part-Timer

The demon lord ends up working at a fast-food restaurant in modern Tokyo, creating a perfect blend of comedy and fish-out-of-water situations.

12. Prison School

A ridiculous comedy about five boys who are the first male students in a former all-girls school, ending up in the school's prison for their misdeeds.

11. Saiki K

Follow the daily struggles of a powerful psychic trying to live a normal life while hiding his abilities from his classmates.

10. One Punch Man

A superhero parody about Saitama, who can defeat any enemy with a single punch, leading to hilarious situations as he searches for a worthy opponent.

9. Asobi Asobase

Three high school girls form a "pastimers club" leading to increasingly bizarre and funny situations.

8. Daily Lives of High School Boys

A hilarious look at the everyday lives of high school boys, filled with relatable and absurd situations.

7. Cromartie High School

A surreal comedy about a normal student in a school full of delinquents, robots, and even Freddie Mercury lookalikes. The deadpan humor and bizarre situations make it a unique comedy experience.

6. Hinamatsuri

A yakuza member's life is turned upside down when a psychokinetic girl suddenly appears in his apartment.

5. Great Teacher Onizuka

An ex-gang member becomes a teacher and uses unconventional methods to help his students, resulting in both heartwarming and hilarious moments.

4. Konosuba

A hilarious isekai parody following Kazuma and his dysfunctional party members in a fantasy world.

3. Gintama

Set in an alternate Edo period where aliens have invaded Earth, follow the misadventures of samurai Gintoki Sakata and his friends.

2. Grand Blue

A "diving" comedy that follows college student Iori Kitahara's misadventures with the diving club, which seems more interested in drinking than actual diving.

1. Nichijou

The ultimate comedy anime that perfectly blends surreal humor with everyday situations, enhanced by stunning animation and perfect comedic timing.

Conclusion

These comedy anime series represent the best of what the genre has to offer, each bringing its own unique style of humor and entertainment. Whether you prefer subtle jokes or over-the-top comedy, there's something here for everyone.
